How It Works

How We Find Your Deals

No single logistics worker can walk into a national retailer and negotiate a discount. But thousands of us, together, can. That's the whole idea behind Logicard.

Group Buying Power

Strength in numbers

Logicard exists to represent logistics workers as a single, collective group — because that's what actually gets a brand's attention.

One voice, thousands strong

On your own, a discount request goes nowhere. As part of a network of thousands of logistics workers, Logicard can ask on behalf of everyone at once.

Direct brand partnerships

We approach retailers and service providers directly, rather than relying on generic public offers anyone can find online.

Bigger group, better rates

As Logicard's membership grows, so does our negotiating position — we go back to partners regularly to push for stronger discounts.

Checked before it's listed

Every offer is compared against genuine market pricing before it goes live, so the saving on the label is a real one.

The Process

From new member to negotiated deal

The same collective process runs behind every deal on Logicard.

1

The group grows

Every new member who joins Logicard adds to the collective size of the group we represent to brands.

2

We approach partners

Our team reaches out to retailers and service providers relevant to the everyday spend of logistics workers.

3

We negotiate as one

Instead of one person asking for a discount, we ask on behalf of the whole Logicard membership.

4

You get the deal

The negotiated rate goes straight into your member account, ready to use whenever you need it.

The bigger Logicard grows, the stronger our negotiating position becomes — every new member helps secure a better deal for everyone else too.

Quality Over Quantity

Not every offer makes the cut

A discount is only worth listing if it's a genuine one.

Real market comparison — we check the discounted price against genuine retail pricing, not an inflated "before" price.

Relevant to logistics workers — offers are chosen around the day-to-day life of drivers, warehouse teams and transport staff, not generic discounts.

Kept up to date — offers are reviewed regularly and removed if a partner can no longer honour the terms.

Exclusive to members — verified Logicard members only, keeping the group genuine and the deals meaningful.
